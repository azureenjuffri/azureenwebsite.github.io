My Family

My Mother and My Late Father
My Small Family in 2012

          The head of our family is my father. His word is law for all. My father's name Ahmad Juffri Bin Abdul Latiff, He passed away in 2019 at the age of 58 years old in front of my eyes when im taking SPM for that year. He never complained of being tired after work. He is my inspiration. He inspires me to be patient in the face of any challenge that comes my way.He raised our 3 siblings without complaining any words for him.

Besides that, Fatimah Binti Awang is my mother's given name. My mother's are the one looks the house after my dad passed away. She gets up really early. She does not rely on the maid for the cook and cleaning the house. She is a lovely lady. She is somewhat conservative but not narrow minded. She is strong and courageous. My father has a great regard for her. He never does anything without consulting her. My mother is truly my inspiration for her strengthen.

I am the youngest daughter of my parents. I have 2 sisters out of 3 siblings. My eldest sister name Nur 'Aqilah and the second sister name Nur A'syikin. Both of them already get married and have children. My first sister already finished study at Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS) in Bachelor of Science (ResourcesChemistry). My second sister also already finished her study at UiTM Kampus Seremban 3 in Bachelor of Science Computer and Mathematics. I am very grateful and hope to Allah SWT that my family is blessed and abundant with sustenance as wide as the ocean and may Allah make all our journeys easier. No words can describe how much I love my family.

UiTM Dihatiku.
